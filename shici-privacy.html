Privacy Policy — 读诗 (Chinese Poetry)

Last updated: 1 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the 读诗 (Chinese Poetry) mobile application ("the App"), published by Shenbee ("we", "us"), handles information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. No account, no sign-in

The App does not require you to create an account or provide any name, email address, or phone number. You can use the App without identifying yourself.

2. Information stored on your device

Your preferences and activity — favorites, playlists, playback settings, and whether you have unlocked the full catalog — are stored locally on your device only (via the operating system's local storage). We do not collect, transmit, or have access to this information. Uninstalling the App removes it.

3. Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock all poems. Purchases are processed by Google Play and managed through RevenueCat, Inc., our purchase-infrastructure provider. To validate and restore your purchase, RevenueCat receives a randomly generated app-user identifier and the purchase/transaction details provided by the store. We never receive or store your credit-card or payment details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

4. No advertising

The App contains no advertising and does not use the device advertising identifier.

5. Children

The App is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. It does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Data security & retention

Because preferences stay on your device and we operate no user accounts, there is no personal data held on our servers. Purchase records held by Google and RevenueCat are retained according to their respective policies.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at info@shenbee.com.
