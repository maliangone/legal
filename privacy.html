Privacy Policy

App: Mindful Reminder  |  Developer: Shenbee  |  Effective: May 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Mindful Reminder does not directly collect personal information. The app stores your notification preferences (schedule, frequency, custom messages) locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (free version only)
AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to serve and personalize advertisements. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat (in-app purchases)
RevenueCat processes purchase receipts to validate your in-app purchase and enable purchase restoration. It may receive your device identifier and purchase history. Governed by RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Information

  • Deliver local notifications on your device at your configured times.
  • Validate and restore in-app purchases.
  • Serve advertisements in the free version of the app.

4. Data Retention

All app preferences are stored locally and are deleted when you uninstall the app. RevenueCat retains purchase records to support purchase restoration. Google AdMob retains data according to Google's data retention policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Mindful Reminder is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by going to your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization. Alternatively, purchasing "Remove Ads" will completely remove all advertisements from the app.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
