Privacy Policy — 小小汉字 (Hanzi Kids)

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the 小小汉字 (Hanzi Kids) mobile application ("the App"), published by Shenbee ("we", "us"), handles information. The App is an offline Chinese-character learning tool for children. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. No account, no sign-in

The App does not require you to create an account or provide any name, email address, or phone number. You can use the App without identifying yourself.

2. Information stored on your device

The App works fully offline. Your child's learning progress, favorites, practice records, and settings are stored locally on your device only (via the operating system's local storage). We do not collect, transmit, or have access to this information. Uninstalling the App removes it.

3. Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full experience. Purchases are processed by Google Play and managed through RevenueCat, Inc., our purchase-infrastructure provider. To validate and restore your purchase, RevenueCat receives a randomly generated app-user identifier and the purchase/transaction details provided by the store. We never receive or store your credit-card or payment details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Handwriting recognition

The trace-to-write practice feature uses Google's on-device ML Kit Digital Ink Recognition. The first time the feature is used, the App downloads a Chinese handwriting model through Google Play services; thereafter it runs entirely on your device. Strokes your child draws are processed locally and are never uploaded to us.

5. No advertising

The App contains no advertising and does not use the device advertising identifier. It does not track your child across apps or websites.

6. Children

The App is designed for children aged 4–10 and is family-friendly. It does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Consistent with Google Play's Families policy and applicable children's-privacy laws, the App collects no personal data and serves no ads.

7. Data security & retention

Because all learning data stays on your device and we operate no user accounts, there is no personal data held on our servers. Purchase records held by Google and RevenueCat are retained according to their respective policies.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at info@shenbee.com.
