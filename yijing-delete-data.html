Data Deletion — 易问 (Augur)

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of data associated with the 易问 (Augur) app, published by Shenbee. See our Privacy Policy for full details on what we store.

1. Delete on-device data

If you used the App offline without signing in, your casting history is stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling the App permanently erases it.

2. Delete your account and cloud data

If you signed in with Google, we store your account (email, display name, account identifier) and your divination records (cast hexagrams, questions, and interpretation conversations) in our cloud database. To delete your account and all associated cloud data:

  1. Email us at info@shenbee.com from the Google account email you signed in with, with the subject line Delete my account — Augur.
  2. We will permanently delete your account record and all associated divination data within 30 days and confirm by email.

3. Delete purchase-related data

If you subscribed, a randomly generated app-user identifier and the purchase/transaction record are held by Google Play and by our purchase provider RevenueCat, Inc. (used only to validate and restore subscriptions — never for advertising). Request deletion of that record in the same email; include your Google Play order number if available so we can locate it.

4. What is deleted vs. retained

Deleted: your account record, all cloud-stored divination data, and the RevenueCat app-user record tied to your request; on-device data is removed by uninstalling. Retained: transaction records that Google Play and RevenueCat must keep for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes, in line with their own retention policies.

5. Contact

Questions about data deletion? Email info@shenbee.com.
