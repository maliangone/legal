Data Deletion — 小小汉字 (Hanzi Kids)

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of data associated with the 小小汉字 (Hanzi Kids) app, published by Shenbee. The app is fully offline and does not require an account; it stores your child's learning data only on the device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

1. Delete the data stored on your device

All learning progress, favorites, practice records, and settings are stored locally on your device only. To delete them:

  1. Uninstall the 小小汉字 (Hanzi Kids) app.
  2. Uninstalling permanently and completely erases all local app data.

2. Delete purchase-related data

If you made an in-app purchase, a randomly generated app-user identifier and the purchase/transaction record are held by Google Play and by our purchase provider RevenueCat, Inc. (used only to validate and restore purchases — never for advertising). To request deletion of that record:

  1. Email us at info@shenbee.com from the address used for the request, with the subject line Delete my data — Hanzi Kids.
  2. If available, include your Google Play order number so we can locate the record.
  3. We will delete the associated app-user record within 30 days and confirm by email.

3. What is deleted vs. retained

Deleted: the RevenueCat app-user record tied to your request, and all on-device data (removed by uninstalling). Retained: transaction records that Google Play and RevenueCat must keep for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes, in line with their own retention policies.

4. Contact

Questions about data deletion? Email info@shenbee.com.
